About Me

Hello! I'm an applied econometrician by training, and am currently working as a management consultant with Deloitte India. I'm primarily interested in empirical analysis of banking, business, taxation, and industrial policies.

I received my undergraduate degree from the Chinese University of Hong Kong in business, and my masters degree from the University of Chicago (Harris) in public policy studies.
